Hawker Experience VR

Overview

The Virtual Hawker Experience is an innovative VR app that immerses users in the vibrant world of hawker culture, showcasing a variety of culinary traditions from Singapore's hawker centers. This educational game provides a fun introduction to iconic local dishes and unique hawker customs like 'choping' tables, encouraging players to discover and experience Singapore's rich culinary scene firsthand.

Hawker centers in Singapore represent the nation's multicultural society through a diverse range of dishes. Newton Circle Food Centre, featured in "Crazy Rich Asians," is a popular culinary destination showcasing Singaporean gastronomy and culture, attracting both locals and global visitors alike.

I oversaw the development of database architecture, including authentication and the point system. Moreover, I designed and developed the bird swatting mini-game along with its assets.

Branding

Red and white colours were retained to symbolize Singapore's flag but adjusted the red tone for better readability. Red is known to stimulate appetite and is commonly used in food-related logos. The addition of yellow in the background complements the red and evokes a cheerful and inviting mood, aligning with our goal of creating a fun and educational hawker experience. We modified the tagline to "savour traditions through VR" to clarify the purpose of HEVR and enhance understanding for our audience.

Game

The Virtual Hawker Experience (HEVR) offers an immersive journey into Singaporean hawker culture through interactive games and quizzes. Players begin with engaging in the "Chope Game" to simulate reserving a table using tissue packets, emphasizing the importance of table reservation in crowded hawker centers. Quiz Trivia educates players on hawker center etiquette, including payment methods, takeaway procedures, and local lingo, presented with engaging visual models. The Ordering Section allows players to practice ordering Cai Fan (Rice with Dishes) to familiarize themselves with Singaporean cuisine and ordering customs. In the Buying Drinks segment, players learn essential drink-ordering words and slang. "Myna Catch" challenges players to shoo away birds commonly found at hawker centers while earning points. Lastly, Tray Return teaches players proper tray disposal to avoid breaking the law when visiting hawker centers in Singapore. HEVR's interactive features aim to educate and entertain, encouraging players to explore and appreciate Singapore's rich hawker culture.
