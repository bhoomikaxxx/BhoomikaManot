Whiskered Inquiry

Overview

Whiskered Inquiry is a first-person visual novel game that immerses players in a detective experience, allowing them to search for evidence and engage in courtroom battles to uncover the culprit. Tailored for children aged 12 and above, the game offers interactive storytelling. Every aspect of the game, including design and narrative, is meticulously crafted from scratch to deliver a memorable gaming experience.

The spin-off mobile game, Whiskered Inquiry - Trials and Debate, extends the gaming experience to a convenient mobile platform, ensuring players can seamlessly continue their adventures on the go. Key features include a gacha system for collecting characters and engaging in debate trials. Sustainability is achieved through profits generated from gacha sales, allowing for ongoing development and support of the app.

I lead the overall visual design, such as branding and high fidelity app prototype. Additionally, I contributed to level design and assets.

Branding

The game and app maintain a light-hearted atmosphere, reflected in their playful art style. Furthermore, the use of a cartoon storytelling style pays homage to the game's visual novel concept. Dominated by yellow and blue hues, the colour palette is inspired by the main characters, Rain and Sun.The choice of Kopik Bold font adds to the inviting nature, encouraging user engagement with its fun appeal and enhancing the overall experience.

Applications Used

Unity Autodesk Maya Adobe Illustrator Adobe XD Visual Studio Code

High Fidelity Prototype

Game

The game begins within an office setting, serving as the backdrop for players to acquaint themselves with the character. Presented with the opportunity to gather evidence, players assume the role of defending a crucial client before the jury. Significantly, key segments of the game unfold within the crime scene and courtroom environments. NavMesh AI is employed in the game for the movement of non-player characters.
