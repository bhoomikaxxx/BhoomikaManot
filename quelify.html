Quelify

Overview

Quelify is a real time app that aims to provide a pleasant experience for students and staff to monitor crowd levels, reserve seats and view food options. This is to prevent overcrowding and minimise risk of spreading illnesses, for the safety of others.

This is a solo project. I have done user research and created high fidelity wireframes for a functional app prototype.

Prototype

The fully functional high-fidelity prototype provides users with an immersive preview of our forthcoming app, offering three core features: virtual queue management, seat reservation, and online ordering for takeaway. Enhancements to user experience include the "last visited" feature, allowing users to review their order and queue history. Additionally, for added convenience, a "book again" button facilitates seamless rebooking with just a tap, streamlining the user journey.

Competitive Analysis

Among the three competitors I researched, QuQueue emerges as the winner, boasting the most user-friendly interface with intuitive navigation. Its colour-coded system for various services enhances usability, while personalised greetings add a personal touch. Notably, a bottom-of-screen main menu facilitates swift access to different sections, including the option to join virtual queues via QR code scanning.
