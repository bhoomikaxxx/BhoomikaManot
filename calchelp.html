Calc Help

Equitable Elevation Bronze Winner

Devpost Entry

Overview

CalcHelp is a secure app accessible with the correct equation and answer set by the user. Upon unlocking, users can immediately call emergency services from the homepage, with location services enabled to pinpoint their location for police assistance. The app includes Yes or No questions to help prepare the police, ensuring quick responses from victims. An Incident Report section allows safe users to report incidents with open-ended questions for detailed information. A History page displays past reports and emergency calls, while a Resources section offers mental health and legal assistance to victims in need.

This project is a solo endeavor where I conducted research and developed the high-fidelity prototype independently.
