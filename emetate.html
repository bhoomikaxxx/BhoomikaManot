Emetate

Funded by Impart

Mindful Hacks 23 Finalists


Overview

Project Emetate represents an innovative solution aimed at addressing the prevailing stigma surrounding mental health in Singapore, offered at an affordable price point. Comprising key components such as an informative website, a Mental Health Quiz,and an integrated Artificial Intelligence Chatbot, the initiative leverages cutting-edge technology,employing deep learning algorithms for continuous improvement. Designed with user-friendliness in mind,the game is accessible to individuals of all ages, fostering a more inclusive approach to mental health awareness.

I led the game development for 'Empathia: Journey Through Emotions' for the competition, and I am currently the front-end web developer for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

Mindful Hacks 23 Presentation

Website

The AI (Artificial Intelligence) Chatbot is able to detect human emotions based on the interactions. It will respond accordingly to the feelings, and how they can overcome the negative feelings. If the bot is unable to detect the emotion, it will ask user to define the word. The chatbot then adds the word to the ‘word bank’, for easier accessibility. The bot can also detect the severity of the emotion. Based on the inout, it will provide the best next steps such as doing positive actions, repeating affirmative words and even as far as seeking professional help.
A full-stack live website has been developed as of December 2023. The quiz has 2 features, one for the public and one for the UWC School Community. It advises the user based on the answers, in the form of a dropdown selection. Via the UWC Portal, the student can send the answers to the quiz and evaluated information to the counselors. With the precalculated level of severity from the website, the counselors can easily determine what the student’s emotion is.
