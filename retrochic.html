Retro Chic

Overview

RetroChic, the soon-to-launch vintage club, that aspires to create buzz for the grand opening. It is the brainchild of a 2 member team, for a school assignment.

I lead the overall visual design, such as branding, website and high fidelity app prototype. Additionally, I created the gamification system of the app prototype.

Branding

Our branding concept for the retro vinyl store is a vibrant fusion of nostalgia and futuristic flair, drawing inspiration from the aesthetics of pastel vaporwave. The 90â€™s were all about rebellion and freedom, and has now inspired the youth to be themselves more. The pastel colours signify a sense of calmness, welcoming people of all walks of life. Our logo is a perfect blend of both worlds.

Applications Used

Adobe Illustrator Adobe Photoshop Adobe XD Bootstrap HTML CSS Javascript RestDB

Prototype

The prototype allowed us to make our vision come true. The low and high fidelity prototype, crafted with Adobe XD is a fully functional prototype of the ecommerce app. It incorporates distinctive features such as games and leaderboard strategically designed to captivate our intended demographic, the Achievers.

Website

The website has been meticulously designed to entice customers in anticipation of the grand opening. Our platform boasts unique features including timers, social sharing capabilities, and an interactive quiz. Furthermore, we've seamlessly integrated signup, login, and contact data with RestDB. In terms of design, we've gone above and beyond to captivate our audience, incorporating animations such as flying thunderbolts and typewriting effects.
